Working at Nebulous
Nebulous is the parent company of Sia, and funds the majority of Sia's development. Nebulous is based in Boston, and requires all employees to live within commuting distance from the office. We are looking primarily for full time employees, however are also open to discussing alternate arangements.

You can apply by sending an email to david@nebulouslabs.com

UI/UX Engineer
We are currently looking for a UI/UX engineer and designer. Competence with HTML, CSS, and Javascript is required. For the early stage of the company, you will be in charge of all items pertaining to user experience. This primarily means the UI, but also includes landing pages, tutorials, api, and anything that's a part of the standard consumer or developer experience. If you are applying for this position, please include links to your prior work in your applicaiton. We have a preference for minimalism and meticulous design.

Decentralization Engineer
We are looking for someone to help us build a cutting edge and extremely efficient decentralized network. This includes desigining and implementing algorithms for choosing hosts from the network, designing and implementing algorithms that help hosts to choose prices for their storage and bandwidth, and optimizing software to maximize scalability. Because Sia operates in a decentralized context, you will need to be able to design algorithms that are stable and successful under harsh adversarial environments. This position requires substantial knowledge of cryptocurrency systems, especially Bitcoin and the various proposals that exist for modifications to Bitcoin. General knowledge of distributed systems is also preferred.

Marketing Director
We are looking for someone who can help bring Sia to the market, and can help find the markets most ready to accept Sia. You will manage relationships to consumers and help figure out which features are highest priority. You will also manage relationships with businesses, both those looking for decentralized solutions, and those currently happy with existing solutions. You will have a strong influence over the early featureset of Sia.
